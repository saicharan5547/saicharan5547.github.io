What is E-Waste?

Electronic waste, is the term used to describe old, end-of-life, or discarded electronic devices. It includes items like computers, laptops, mobile phones, televisions, printers, and other electronic equipment.

E-waste is a growing problem around the world. A large amount of e-waste is not disposed of properly, leading to serious environmental and health issues. It's important to manage e-waste effectively to reduce its impact.

How to Identify E-Waste?

Recycling

Recycling e-waste helps in recovering valuable materials for reuse and reduces the environmental impact of disposal.

Reuse

Reuse of electronic devices or their components extends their lifespan and reduces the need for new production.

Proper Disposal

Ensure e-waste is disposed of properly, following regulations to prevent environmental pollution.
